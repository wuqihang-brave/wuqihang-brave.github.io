### English Article
Negentropy, a concept derived from the second law of thermodynamics, symbolizes the emergence of order and organization in contrast to the inevitable decay of energy in all systems. In a universe dominated by entropy, the act of creating structure and coherence can be seen as a form of heroism. This act is not simply the avoidance of chaos, but the proactive pursuit of harmony amidst disorder.

### Chinese Article
